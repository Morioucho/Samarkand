What is Samarkand?

July 10th, 2024

Samarkand is a webapp intended to serve local communities. Samarkand's name is inspired from the similar named city, a major trading hub and marketplace during the prime of the Silk Road. Samarkand led to the spread and diffusion of various cultures and items throughout the world. Likewise, the Samarkand website intends to connect communities and local businesses together.

Samarkand intends to create a variety of features that would benefit the community, these examples are:

  • Direct Business Interaction
  • Food Marketplace
  • Business Marketplace
  • Job Listings
  • Community Posts

With the introduction of these features, Samarkand would be a great option for both growing communities and developed communities. Samarkand also integrates the usage of AI in order to foster connections such as connecting potential job hunters with employers and connecting local businesses with local consumers.
